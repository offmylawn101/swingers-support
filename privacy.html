Swingers Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Overview

Swingers is an offline-first arcade game. The app stores your display name, settings, ad-frequency state, and all-time high score on your device.

Data Collection

Swingers does not require an account and does not transmit your display name, score, or gameplay progress. The app includes Google Mobile Ads and Apple StoreKit.

You can erase this local data from the in-app Settings screen. Deleting the app also removes locally stored game data from the device.

Network Features

Gameplay remains available offline. Swingers does not submit scores, display names, or progress to a leaderboard service. Ads, purchase, restore, and privacy-message features require network access.

Purchases and Advertising

Google Mobile Ads may process IP-derived approximate location, device identifiers, advertising data, ad and product interactions, crash data, and performance data to serve, limit, and measure ads. Review Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

Swingers does not request App Tracking Transparency permission or access the IDFA. Personalized advertising and Google's publisher first-party identifier are disabled. Where required, Google User Messaging Platform presents consent choices and Settings provides an Ad Privacy entry point.

Interstitial ads appear only between some completed runs, never during active gameplay. A one-time Remove Ads Forever purchase is available for USD 0.99. Apple processes the purchase through StoreKit and maintains the entitlement for restore and Family Sharing. Swingers does not use Stripe or collect payment-card details.

After ad removal is purchased or restored, Swingers stops loading and presenting ads. The colorful billboards inside the city are fictional game artwork, not paid placements.

Children

Swingers does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The game does not include chat or user-generated content.

Contact

For privacy or support questions, open a public Swingers support request. Do not include private account information.
