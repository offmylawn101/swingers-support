Swingers Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Overview

Swingers is an offline-first arcade game. The launch build stores your display name, settings, and all-time high score on your device.

Data Collection

The launch build does not include third-party analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, IDFA access, chat, user-generated uploads, or real-money purchases.

You can erase this local data from the in-app Settings screen. Deleting the app also removes locally stored game data from the device.

Network Features

Gameplay remains available offline. This launch build does not submit scores, display names, or progress to a leaderboard service.

Purchases and Advertising

This build has no in-app purchases and does not display third-party advertising. The colorful billboards inside the city are fictional game artwork.

Children

Swingers does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The game does not include chat or user-generated content.

Contact

For privacy or support questions, open a public Swingers support request. Do not include private account information.
