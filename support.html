Swingers Support

Use this page for gameplay help, troubleshooting, and App Review context.

Gameplay

Swingers is a single-player arcade score chase. Hold Swing to grapple, release to fly, and use Dash for a burst of speed. Hit rings, maintain a chain, and smash through buildings to raise your score. Touching the ground ends the run.

Pause and Restart

Use the pause button during a run to resume, restart immediately, or return home. The run recap also lets you swing again or return home.

Offline Play

The core game works offline. Progress is saved on the device.

Your settings, display name, recent run history, and all-time high score stay on this device. Use Reset Local Data in Settings to erase them.

Performance and Accessibility

Settings includes Auto, Battery Saver, and Quality performance profiles, plus Audio, Haptics, Reduce Motion, Color Assist, and an optional Diagnostics overlay. These options do not change scoring.

Troubleshooting

If the game is interrupted, reopen it and start a new run. For sustained frame-rate or heat issues, choose Auto or Battery Saver in Performance settings and close other intensive apps.

Contact

Open a public Swingers support request. Include your iPhone model, iOS version, and a short description of what happened. Do not include private account information.
